Our team:

Founded as a fun project in 1998 by the two chefs Carl and Thomas Burkart the Fancy Food truck blew all their expectations as it got featured in many local food blogs as the best place to get sandwiches from. After only one year of operation the lines infront of the truck grew so large Carl and Thomas had to employ additional staff to satisfy the increasing demand.
Today the team consists of 9 staff members. The two chefs Thomas and Carl, our 2 Food scientists John and Michael, the two secretaries Stefanie and Paul, and also our 3 "Sandwich Makers" Lena, Paula and Minnie
